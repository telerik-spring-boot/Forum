Help

1. Getting Started

Welcome to Roamify! This help section provides guidance on how to use our services effectively.

2. Creating an Account

To register on Roamify:

  • Click on the person icon in the top-right corner.
  • Select "Register" and fill in your details (First Name, Last Name, Email, Username, Password).
  • Click "Sign Up" to create your account.

3. Managing Your Account

Once registered, you can manage your account settings:

  • Click on the person icon in the top-right corner.
  • Select "Settings" to update your details or upload a profile picture.
  • To delete your account, go to "Settings" and choose the option to close your account.

4. Posting and Interacting

Roamify allows users to share travel-related content:

  • On the main page, you can view all user posts and comments.
  • You can comment on posts and interact with other users.
  • Use the search bar to find posts by tags, title, creator, or likes.
  • Sort posts by popularity, date, or engagement.

5. Viewing Your Content

To find your own posts and activity:

  • Click on the person icon in the top-right corner.
  • Select "View Profile" to see your posts and interactions.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our platform:

  • Can I edit my post after publishing? - Yes, navigate to your post and click "Edit".
  • How I edit my comment after publishing? - Yes, navigate to your comment and click "Edit".
  • Can I search users by their username? - Yes, you can use the search bar by typing their username.
  • Can I recover a deleted account? - No, once deleted, accounts cannot be restored.

7. Contact Support

If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team at support@roamify.com.
